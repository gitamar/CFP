Principal's Word

Nadar Mahajana Sangam S.Vellaichamy Nadar College had a humble beginning in 1965. It is one of the prominent Institutions in Tamilnadu after crossing many milestones such as the obtainment of the Autonomy, the status of ISO and ‘A’ Grade in NAAC Accreditation. The primary aim of our college is to offer holistic education to the student community which will pave the way for them to emerge as socially responsible citizens in course of time and render significant contribution to our future India. Besides enlightening the minds of the students in their respective fields, the teaching fraternity of our college makes earnest efforts to inculcate the ethical values in the minds of the students. By doing so, our college students are transformed into noble beings. As the Principal and Head of the Institution, it is my dream and desire that our students should obtain an overall development and reach out to an elevated position in the society, serving the people with a sense of absolute commitment and dedication. Our college obviously aims at making the students people-oriented rather than self-centered. It is a matter of immense joy and pride that our college is marching towards greater heights with the unwavering support of the teaching fraternity and the members of the Management. I am pretty sure that our college will soon emerge as one of the leading Institutions which will be recognized globally.
